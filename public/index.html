Why isn't Plaid pulling these?
Plaid connects to banking infrastructure — it can read your account balance, credit limit, and transactions. But rewards points live in separate loyalty systems (Chase UR, Amex MR, etc.) that are outside Plaid's scope. Auto-sync of points requires the AwardWallet partner API (backlog item BACK-003).
